Giant Sequoia

The Giant Sequoia (Sequoiadendron giganteum) is one of the most massive tree species on Earth. Here are some fascinating facts about them:

  • Height: They can grow over 300 feet tall.
  • Age: Some sequoias are over 3,000 years old.
  • Location: They are primarily found in the Sierra Nevada mountains of California.
  • Diameter: Their trunks can reach diameters of over 30 feet.
  • Fire Resistance: Their thick bark helps protect them from forest fires.

Giant Sequoias are a symbol of longevity and resilience, making them a popular attraction for nature enthusiasts and tourists alike.
